A giraffe unicycle..

When life's a circus, why not ride a unicycle and make a fool of yourself? When I was young, I had the opportunity to live in Australia. While in Australia, I attended a circus camp and fell in love. It started with juggling, then went to fire juggling. Next, I moved to a unicycle, and shortly after jumped onto the giraffe unicycle above. I made myself stilts only to later buy jumping stilts. I'm a total clown in more ways than just a love for all things circus.

Love is another colossal priority in my life. My friends and family mean the world to me. I try to call my mom at least once a week and my dad the same. I have two older brothers and a younger sister. Before fall term 2020, I was a cook and had been a cook my entire working life. With the lockdown came the loss of my job. So I decided to head back to school for Computer Information Systems.
