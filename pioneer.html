Who's Steve Woznaik

Co-founder of Apple

Steve Wozniak, or better known as simply "Woz," was a major player in the personal computer revolution. While IBM was building computers for the business market, Woz and his friend Steve Jobs, where working on a more consumer friendly variety. In 1975 he started working on what would become the Apple I in 1976 and followed up with the Apple II; which would come to be known as the first mass produced microcomputer. Prior to Apple, Wozniak was expelled from Boulder in 1967 when he hacked into the schools computer system. Setting up a life destined for technological discovery. While Jobs is often credited for much of Apples success, Woz was the innovation behind the marketing campaign. "Between Woz and Jobs, Woz was the innovator, the inventor.
